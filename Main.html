About Me

I'm a junior studying Electrical Engineering at the University of Alabama with a deep passion for biotechnology and its potential to transform healthcare. Along with a major in electrical engineering, I have minors in Biochemistry and Engineering Positive and Intentional Change (EPIC), which reflect my dedication to merging technical innovation with social impact. When I’m not in the lab or the classroom, you’ll often find me baking something new, diving into a good book, or writing as a creative outlet. I believe that curiosity, compassion, and a strong work ethic are at the heart of engineering.
